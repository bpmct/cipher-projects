Encript a plaintext into binary using a keyphrase!





How it works

This algorithm works to convert a plaintext and a keyphrase into binary and then use a binary substitution cipher to make a not-that-secure cipher from your inputs. It will automatically match your keyphrase length to the plaintext to ensure the best possible transcription!

Note: It's case sensitive and only works with letters of the English alphabet!
